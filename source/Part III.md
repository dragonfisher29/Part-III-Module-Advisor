## Description

The major component of the third year is the Individual Project (ECSP3000/ECSP3001), which runs across both semesters. In Part III, students also take 75 credits (37.5 ECTS) of optional modules from the list below, for a total load of 60 credits (30 ECTS) per semester. They may select at most 30 credits (15 ECTS) worth of options from outside the programme. Broadening options may be chosen from the following list only: 
- MATH3081 Operational Research 
- MATH3082 Optimisation 
- PHYS2015 Introduction to Energy in The Environment 
- SSPC2012 Gender and Society 
- UOSM2004 Global Health 
- UOSM2017 Intercultural Communication in a Global World 
- UOSM2022 Social Enterprise 
- UOSM2026 Ethics in Science, Engineering and Technology 
- UOSM2043 Global Sustainability Challenges
